PENTAGRAM - a game where magic is trap-based
Form pentagrams and other shapes by placing down runes in specific arrangements.
Activate them to cast magic. The type of rune affects what kind of magic is cast.
Beware: If your pentagram is too misshapen, your spell may backfire!
Spell types may vary from explosions to stuns to heals to summons.

Current controls:
Use arrow keys to move.
Press t to place a rune down.
Form a shape, and press space to connect the last line to activate the pentagram.
Damage varies based on the shape.
A maximum of 6 runes may be placed.
